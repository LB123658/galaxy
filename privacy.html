Galaxy Search Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 22, 2021


     Galaxy Search never tracks you on its website and does not use cookies or keep track of your search history in any way.

     Websites that come up in the search results, however, may have a lot of trackers or may be unsafe because Galaxy cannot check to make sure they are all safe.

     Galaxy never tracks search history, location, or anything else about you. Any stored information is stored only in your browser so no one else can access it. More features will be added to Galaxy soon.
